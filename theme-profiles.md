# Natelly Theme Profiles

These brand directions are based on your description of Natelly as:

- innovation meets fun
- good people
- new ideas
- open mindedness

## 1. Playful Lab

**Positioning:** Creative intelligence with a human face.  
**Brand feeling:** Curious, bright, inventive, welcoming.  
**Why it fits:** This direction makes Natelly feel like a company where smart people build exciting things together without sounding stiff or over-corporate.

**Color direction**
- Deep ocean blue `#0C3B66`
- Bright teal `#13B6B1`
- Sun yellow `#FFD578`
- Soft coral pink `#FF89AE`

**Typography**
- Headings: Space Grotesk
- Body: DM Sans

**Visual language**
- Rounded cards
- Colorful gradients
- Layered shapes and soft glow accents
- Friendly but sharp messaging

**Sample voice**
"Big ideas grow faster in good company."

## 2. Open House

**Positioning:** A welcoming home for smart, open-minded collaboration.  
**Brand feeling:** Inclusive, expressive, optimistic, people-first.  
**Why it fits:** Best if you want visitors to immediately feel Natelly is a place for connection, conversation, and fresh thinking.

**Color direction**
- Electric violet `#7657FF`
- Rose `#DB5A82`
- Apricot `#FF9868`
- Warm cream `#FFF3EC`

**Typography**
- Headings: Sora
- Body: Instrument Sans

**Visual language**
- Soft gradients
- Big spacing
- Warm editorial photography direction
- Inviting copy and community cues

**Sample voice**
"New ideas land better when everyone feels invited."

## 3. Bright Atelier

**Positioning:** Clean design thinking with creative warmth.  
**Brand feeling:** Fresh, thoughtful, modern, uplifting.  
**Why it fits:** This is the most refined and airy option. It works well if you want Natelly to feel polished and premium while still being approachable.

**Color direction**
- Mist green `#EBF6D1`
- Soft mint `#8FD6B4`
- Bright aqua `#2EB6B0`
- Deep evergreen `#173029`

**Typography**
- Headings: Manrope
- Body: IBM Plex Sans

**Visual language**
- Clean sections
- Light backgrounds with crisp contrast
- Minimal but lively accent color use
- Elegant layout rhythm

**Sample voice**
"Build boldly, stay kind, keep moving."

## 4. Midnight Club

**Positioning:** A confident, idea-led brand with cultural energy.  
**Brand feeling:** Bold, dynamic, magnetic, sharp.  
**Why it fits:** Best for a more striking launch presence. It keeps the fun, but gives Natelly a stronger edge and a more editorial tone.

**Color direction**
- Midnight navy `#112038`
- Royal slate `#1A3560`
- Electric mint `#75FAD6`
- Warm peach `#FFC07B`

**Typography**
- Headings: Syne
- Body: General Sans

**Visual language**
- High contrast panels
- Bright accent highlights
- Confident headlines
- More dramatic composition

**Sample voice**
"Where sharp ideas meet magnetic energy."

## Recommendation

If you want the broadest, strongest starting point for a company website, choose **Playful Lab**.

If you want Natelly to feel more human and community-centered, choose **Open House**.
