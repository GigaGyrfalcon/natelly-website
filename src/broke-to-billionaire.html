Active Project

Broke to Billionaire

A progression-centered game about starting small, building momentum, and making growth feel visible. The core appeal is ambition, long-term advancement, and the satisfaction of seeing effort compound over time.

Core loop at a glance

Tap, upgrade, climb the ladder, then prestige and start stronger.

Summary

What the project is about

Broke to Billionaire is a motivational, progression-based game for people who enjoy building from almost nothing toward something bigger. It is built to feel ambitious and rewarding, with a clear sense of upward movement from early survival to exaggerated wealth milestones.

Why it works

Core appeal

The project taps into the psychology of progress. It speaks to users who like visible milestones, compounding wins, and the emotional lift that comes from feeling like effort is turning into momentum. The game makes that clear through taps, passive income, prestige resets, unlock ladders, and escalating stage names.
