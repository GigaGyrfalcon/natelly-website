Broke to Billionaire

Broke to Billionaire is a progression-based mobile game by Natelly. It combines tapping, upgrades, passive income, and prestige resets to turn visible effort into long-term momentum, and it is now available on the App Store.

Summary

A mobile game about visible progress.

Broke to Billionaire is a motivational mobile idle game for people who enjoy building from almost nothing toward something bigger. It is designed to feel ambitious and rewarding, with a clear sense of upward movement from early survival to exaggerated wealth milestones.

Screens

Current product snapshots.

These screens show the game loop in action: a tap-to-grow economy, upgrade shop, world stage ladder, run tracking, and prestige system. Swipe or scroll across the gallery to explore.

Capital District / Shop the Empire

The shop screen frames the core economy clearly: cash on hand, gems, income per second, and upgrades like Better Shoes, Coffee Habit, and Sales Confidence.

Core Loop / Make Money

The main play screen makes the core loop obvious right away: hold to stack, watch income build, and buy quick upgrades that accelerate the run.

Progress / Track the Climb

World Ladder / Stage Timeline

The stage ladder gives the fantasy structure, moving from Barely Getting By to Professional Success, Wealth Builder, Elite Lifestyle, and beyond.

Prestige Chamber / Royal Prestige

The prestige screen adds the long-term strategy layer by letting players reset the empire, keep permanent bonuses, and start the next run stronger.
