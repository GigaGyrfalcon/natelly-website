Audience Variant / Kind Current

Ideas that move people should feel good to be around.

Inspired by a kindness and good-deeds app, this direction favors gentle curves, calm optimism, and designs that feel emotionally safe, sincere, and active rather than sentimental.

Design language here is softer and more human, with circles and flowing capsules instead of rigid structures.

Shape principle

Flowing capsules, circles, conversation bubbles, and calm layered surfaces that suggest generosity and care.

Primary shapes

Circles, soft capsules, floating message cards, gentle wave divisions.

Typography

Warm but clean headlines with relaxed body copy and a very readable rhythm.

Design principle

Sincerity without blandness. The page should feel kind, not childish or vague.

Emotional tone

Encouraging, grounded, restorative, emotionally intelligent.
