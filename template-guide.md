# Natelly Template Guide

These templates all start from the `Open House` idea, but each one is tuned to a different audience energy.

## 1. Modern Hospitality

**Audience vibe:** Broadly welcoming, thoughtful, creative, people-first.  
**Shapes:** Arches, rounded frames, layered panels.  
**Type feeling:** Open, polished, calm.  
**Best if:** Natelly should feel like an inviting company brand first.

## 2. Play Loop

**Inspired by:** People who enjoy idle games, progress loops, systems, playful rewards.  
**Shapes:** Badges, progress bars, stacked cards, tilted rounded blocks.  
**Type feeling:** Energetic, modular, bright.  
**Best if:** You want Natelly to feel clever, lively, and momentum-driven.

## 3. Kind Current

**Inspired by:** People drawn to kindness habits, community care, meaningful daily actions.  
**Shapes:** Circles, capsules, flowing cards, soft floating surfaces.  
**Type feeling:** Gentle, readable, emotionally warm.  
**Best if:** You want Natelly to feel deeply human, restorative, and values-led.

## 4. Rally Room

**Inspired by:** People drawn to feminist community, solidarity, conversation, and purpose.  
**Shapes:** Bold arches, placards, banners, stacked statement blocks.  
**Type feeling:** Strong, expressive, collective.  
**Best if:** You want Natelly to feel more confident, cultural, and voice-driven.

## My Recommendation

Start with **Modern Hospitality** as the main Natelly foundation.

Then borrow:

- `Play Loop` for interactive details and visual energy
- `Kind Current` for warmth and people-centered tone
- `Rally Room` for confidence and conviction in key sections
