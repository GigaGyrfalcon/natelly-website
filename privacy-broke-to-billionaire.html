Broke to Billionaire

This privacy policy applies to Broke to Billionaire and explains how information may be collected, used, and handled in connection with the game.

Policy scope

This page covers the current project website, app, and related services.

Questions

For privacy questions, reach out to info@natelly.com.

Effective date

March 30, 2026

Overview

Broke to Billionaire is a project by Natelly. This privacy policy explains, at a general level, how information may be collected, used, and handled in connection with the project website, app, or related services.

Information we may collect

Depending on how the project is released, Broke to Billionaire may collect information such as account details, device information, usage activity, gameplay progress, support messages, and analytics data needed to understand performance and improve the experience.

How information may be used

Information may be used to operate the project, support account features, measure product performance, improve gameplay and user experience, respond to support requests, maintain security, and meet legal obligations where required.

Sharing

Natelly may work with service providers that help with hosting, analytics, communications, payments, customer support, or technical operations. Information may be shared with those providers only as needed to support the project or where required by law.

Data retention and security

Information may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the project, resolve disputes, enforce terms, or meet legal requirements. Natelly intends to use reasonable administrative and technical measures to help protect information, but no method of storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your questions

If you have questions about this privacy policy or would like to contact Natelly about Broke to Billionaire, email info@natelly.com.
